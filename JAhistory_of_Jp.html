The History of JP Maid Cafe

JP Made Cafe history dates back to 1986, where founder Marco Trejo created the JP Maid brand with just 18 workers. The first cafe shop was introduced a month later that year, inspired by the Japanese culture of cafe, and were then produced a small cafe of his own. A year later, Johny Flores as the biggest impact when he produces many differrent models of product that deliver strong friendly environment and offer a variety of unique comfort and convenience atmostphere inside and outside the shop that distinguish the JP Maid brand.


Nearly 32 years later, the JP Maid Coffeehouse is still particular about how we serve customers and community, how we innovate and who we hire. As the coffee chain that inspired by the Japanese culture, we can't disappointing them by meet our customer's changind needs by making fresh smell and relax atmostphere. This is the story of all the people in our history who helped change the way we serve. People who continue to make a differrence in the lives of our customers every day.
